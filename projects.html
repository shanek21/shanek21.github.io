Projects

Mobius Gear Strip

I am currently working on creating a fully functioning, 3D printed, servo driven Mobius gear strip. In order to get the Mobius strip to both spin around and rotate along its circular axis, I am going to use helical planeary gear bearings that I custom design. If I can get it to function fully, then it would be a very exciting proof to the power of 3D printing!
