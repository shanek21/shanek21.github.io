Experience

MITRE Corporation - Software Engineering Intern

In the summer of 2017 I was an intern at MITRE Corporation, a not-for-profit company that does federally funded research and development. I worked alongside full-time engineers doing C++ development for a classified government project at the intersection of cyber security and embedded systems.

Raytheon BBN Technologies - Sensor Systems Intern

Over the summer of 2016 I was working as an intern at Raytheon BBN Technologies in the sensor systems department. At BBN, I got the chance to test and write software (in C++ and C) for real-time embedded systems.

Pharos Labs LLC - Software Development Intern

During the summer of 2015 I worked at Pharos Labs LLC writing software for a wide variety of things. I created the interface and back end of data-gathering tools for the company's use, and also worked on data analysis, web crawling tools, and even some web dev.

Olin College - 3D Printing Student Teacher

Since my first semester of my undergraduate degree (and until my last), I have helped manage the 3D printing resources at Olin College. This includes teaching other Olin College students how to use 3D printers, helping students with CAD, and maintaining the 3D printers.

Projects

Genetic Algorithms: Teaching Robots to Learn Complex Tasks

A team of two other students and I created an implementation of a genetic algorithm to teach groups of robots to complete complex cooperative and competitive tasks. Check out our code, our website, and a video of the robots in action.

Autonomous Multi-Floor Building Exploration

A team and I worked towards equipping an existing robot (the robot Mystery Machine seen below) to explore an unknown building, create a map of the building, and use elevators to explore multiple floors - all autonomously. Check out our code and a video displaying some of the robot's capabilities.

Video Game AI

Three friends and I are creating a bot that can play a browser-based video game called Tagpro. Check out our code.

Interactive Wearable Art Piece

I worked on the electronics, CAD, and served as a 3D printing expert on a team of 5 students as we worked to make something meaningful in the realm of wearable science fiction. Our website is here and a video of our wearable being worn can be found here.

Autonomous Robot Mystery Machine

A team and I spent a semester creating a robot that raced in a series of four autonomous robot races at Olin College. We placed first in each of the four race categories. Check out our code and a written report that details my contributions to the design and build process.

Jimmy, the Humanoid Robot

I spent a semester at Olin researching and developing more seamless modes of interaction beteween humans and robots. During this semester of research I wrote a function using python and ROS that allowed Jimmy to move his head to follow your face when you spoke to him. Check out my code here.

Web Enabled Lighting

My roommate and I strung multi-color LED lights around the perimeter of our dorm room and enabled them to be controlled from a website. Here's a link to our git repo.

3D Scanner

A partner and I designed, built, and wrote firmware for a fully functional 360 degree 3D scanner. It runs off of an Arduino Uno, an infrared distance sensor, and two servos. The data it collects is then passed through serial to a computer and visualized in 3D. See a video of it working here.

Computer Vision Motion Prediction

Two teammates and I wrote software to detect unique objects from a video stream and visualize a prediction of its future position. Check out our code and a gif of it working.

Analog Control of a DC Motor

A teammate and I implemented position control for a DC motor without any added sensors. Here is our writeup detailing the circuit and the math behind it all.

Computer Vision Drawing Application

A teammate and I built a webcam-based drawing application using a computer vision library called openCV. You can choose your drawing color by picking up any colorful object around you. Check out our code.
